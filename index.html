Building India’s Next Generation of Filmmakers — On Your Campus

Shorts Cinemaghar is a campus-rooted cinematic learning platform that introduces students to the art of visual storytelling through curated short film screenings, filmmaking workshops, mentorship, and guided film creation.

Powered by Short Film Tour, India’s pioneering offline platform dedicated to showcasing independent short films and connecting filmmakers with audiences.

Why Does This Matter Now?

Students today grow up surrounded by visual media. Stories are consumed through short videos, streaming platforms, and social media every day. Yet while students constantly engage with visual content, very few receive structured guidance on how stories are created, interpreted, and communicated responsibly.

Educational institutions increasingly recognize the importance of creativity, communication, collaboration, and media literacy alongside traditional academics. Cinema provides one of the most powerful mediums through which students can explore these skills.

Bridging the Gap

Shorts Cinemaghar bridges this gap by bringing cinematic learning directly to campuses through structured storytelling experiences.

A 360° Cinematic Ecosystem

What is Shorts Cinemaghar?

Shorts Cinemaghar is India’s first campus-rooted cinematic ecosystem designed to nurture emerging storytellers within educational institutions. Rather than a one-time event, it is designed as a year-round creative ecosystem.

How does the Program Work?

Shorts Cinemaghar follows a structured creative journey. This journey takes students from classroom learning to national recognition.

02

  • Workshops and masterclasses
  • Filmmaking labs

03

Creation Phase

04

  • National tour screenings
  • Digital distribution
  • Awards and recognition
