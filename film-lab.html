What Makes This Film Lab Different?

Most short film competitions reward films that are already completed. The Shorts Cinemaghar Film Lab supports filmmakers before the film exists.

The selected filmmaker will work with the Shorts Cinemaghar team to develop, produce, and launch the film through a structured process including production funding, mentorship, distribution, and digital release with 50% revenue share.

What the Selected Filmmaker Receives?

Production Grant

₹3,00,000 funding to produce the film.

Creative & Production Supervision

Guidance on budgeting, production planning, and execution.

Marketing & Launch Support

Trailer release, posters, social media campaign, and promotional support.

Official Digital Premiere

The film will be released on the Shorts Cinemaghar YouTube Channel.

Revenue Share

50% revenue share with the filmmaker from YouTube revenue.
