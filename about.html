About Us

Shorts Cinemaghar is a cinematic learning initiative designed to introduce students to the art and craft of storytelling through short films.

Who We Are

The initiative creates a bridge between education and the film industry by bringing screenings, mentorship, and filmmaking experiences directly to campuses.

Tagline: Start Small. Dream Big.

Vision

To nurture a generation of thoughtful storytellers by making cinematic learning accessible across educational institutions.

Mission

  • ✔ Introduce students to visual storytelling
  • ✔ Encourage creative collaboration
  • ✔ Connect campuses with filmmakers
  • ✔ Build a nationwide storytelling ecosystem

Screening Powered by Short Film Tour

Shorts Cinemaghar screening is powered by Short Film Tour, India’s pioneering offline short film screening platform.
