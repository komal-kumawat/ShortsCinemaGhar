Shorts Cinemaghar
for Schools

Shorts Cinemaghar – Schools Edition introduces students to storytelling through a structured creative program designed for Classes VIII–XII.

Students create short films using mobile devices and present them during a curated school showcase.

Program Journey

1

Inspiration

Engaging workshops and curated screenings to spark ideas and understand the basics of storytelling.

2

Creation

Guided support for students to shoot, edit, and finalize their own short films using mobile devices.

3

Showcase

A grand school-wide screening event to celebrate student creativity.
